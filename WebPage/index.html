Ethiopian Bank Forex Data Tables

This website has tables of:

  • The black market rate for USD as of the date specified
  • Highest rates, lowest rates and other stats for currencies traded across 7 national banks
  • The exchange rates for currencies across 7 national banks
  • The currencies filtered by transaction type across 7 national banks
  • Aggregate of all the currencies and banks

This information is obtained from official bank websites and is updated daily. Official websites:

  • Awash Bank
  • CBE
  • COOP
  • Dashen Bank
  • Hijra Bank
  • NIB International Bank
  • Wegagen Bank

This data is from the perspective of the bank. For example, Cash Buying means the price at which the bank buys that currency. Therefore, if you are selling $1 in Cash, you would go to the bank with the highest Cash Buying Rate and avoid the bank with the lowest rate.
